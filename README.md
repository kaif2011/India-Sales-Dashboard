# India-Sales-Dashboard
An interactive Power BI report designed to track revenue against targets, regional trends, and category-wise sales across India.

## 📌 Project Overview
The India Sales Performance Dashboard was developed to track business efficiency and revenue drivers across various states. Beyond just visualization, the project focuses on performance gaps—analyzing why the total sales reached 589.65K against a 629K target. By leveraging Power BI, I identified Maharashtra as the leading revenue contributor and Phones as the top-performing product category. This dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor seasonal peaks in June and September and evaluate team efficiency to drive data-backed growth strategies.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
- 📊 Power BI Desktop – Used for building the interactive report and high-level visualizations.  
- 📂 Power Query – Performed extensive data cleaning, handled missing values, and formatted date/state columns.  
- 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Developed custom measures for Total Sales, Profit Margin, and Target Variance to track KPIs accurately.  
- 🔗 Data Modeling – Established a Star Schema relationship between tables to ensure slicers (Region, Category, Manager) work seamlessly.  
- 📁 File Format – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard preview.

## 📁 Data Source
The dataset used contains structured sales records across India, featuring metrics such as Sales Amount, Profit, Quantity, Customer Segments, and State-wise performance data.

## 🚀 Business Context & Goals

### 🔸 Business Problem:
Many businesses struggle to track performance across multiple regions and product categories using raw data alone. For this project, the core challenge was identifying why the business fell 6% short of its 629K sales target. I built this dashboard to pinpoint revenue leakages and highlight high-growth regions like Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh.

---

### 🔸 Goal of the Dashboard:

The objective was to build an interactive dashboard that:

- Tracks Performance: Provides a clear view of overall sales ($589.65K), profit ($129.07K), and total quantity (6K).

- Regional Benchmarking: Compares performance across Indian states to identify top-performing areas and those needing intervention.

- Segment Analysis: Deep-dives into customer segments and product categories like Phones and Bookcases to understand revenue drivers.

- Actionable Insights: Enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions to bridge the performance gap in the upcoming quarters. 

---

---

### 🔸 Business Impact & Insights

- Strategic Planning: Identified a 6% gap between actual sales (589.65K) and the target (629K), providing a clear starting point for performance reviews.

- Regional Focus: Pinpointed Maharashtra as a high-revenue zone, suggesting that similar marketing strategies could be replicated in lower-performing states.

- Inventory Optimization: Trend analysis showed peaks in June and September, helping the business plan stock levels more efficiently for high-demand months.

- Product Growth: Revealed that Phones and Bookcases drive the most revenue, while Accessories show an opportunity for expansion.

- Team Accountability: Provided a transparent view of team lead performance, enabling data-driven appraisals and coaching.
